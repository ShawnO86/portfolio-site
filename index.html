Hello and welcome to my portfolio!

img of me
Hello! My name is Shawn;
I recently graduated with an associate's degree in web programming. I am passionate about designing and developing responsive, user-friendly, functional, and aesthetically pleasing websites and applications that enhance the user experience.

As a certified front-end web developer and responsive web designer , I have gained knowledge and experience in various programing languages related to the web, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL and the basics of PHP. I am also learning to use popular front-end frameworks such as Vue.js and SASS, which will enable me to create dynamic, interactive, and scalable web applications.

Apart from my web development skills, I am also a dedicated family person who enjoys spending quality time with my loved ones. I have a dog and an aquarium, which I enjoy taking care of, and I love to cook and experiment with different cuisines. I also find gardening to be a relaxing and fulfilling activity, and I take pleasure in growing my vegetables and bonsai trees.

I am also a passionate gamer who loves to play and explore different games in my free time. As a lifelong learner, I always stay updated with the latest web development trends and best practices to deliver high-quality projects that meet or exceed my clients' expectations.

Thank you for taking the time to learn a bit about me. Please feel free to explore my portfolio and Github and reach out if you have any questions or opportunities for collaboration.
