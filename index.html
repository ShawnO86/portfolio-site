About Me

Hello! I'm Shawn.

A former house painter transitioning into a career in web development.

As soon as I realized that I would be happier in a tech field that would challenge me intellectually and creatively, I decided to pursue a degree in web programming. I have since obtained an Associateâ€™s Degree in web programming and certifications in responsive web design and front-end development, which have equipped me with the ability to create beautiful, functional & efficient websites that enhance user experiences.

Drawing from my experience as a house painter, I bring several valuable skills to my new career.
  • An eye for detail - which is critical in web development where precision and accuracy are essential.
  • Creativity - another valuable skill honed as a painter is being creative with colors that work well together.
  • Adaptability - unpredictable situations constantly arise in painting and transition well to development as developers must keep up with the latest trends and technologies.
  • Time & Project Management - painters work under tight deadlines and budgets.
  • Interpersonal Skill - working with customers as a painter developed my communication skills, which are necessary to understand client needs and set expectations effectively.
