About Me

Hello! My name is Shawn.

I am passionate about technology and its potential to transform the way we live, work and communicate. There's something about discovering new technologies and understanding how they work that just excites me to no end. As a child, I would often take apart gadgets just to see how they were built, and as I grew older, I started exploring programming languages and web design. Technology is a field that keeps evolving, and I love keeping up with the latest trends and developments.

At the same time, I am also a lover of nature. Being surrounded by the beauty of the natural world is both calming and rejuvenating for me. I enjoy taking long walks in the park or hiking in the mountains to appreciate the wonders of nature. It reminds me of how small we are in the grand scheme of things and that we need to take care of our environment for future generations.

When I'm not tinkering with technology or exploring nature, you can usually find me in the kitchen, cooking up a storm. There's nothing quite like the feeling of creating something delicious from scratch. I enjoy experimenting with new recipes and techniques, and I find cooking to be both therapeutic and rewarding.

My journey towards technology was a bit unconventional. I started out as a house painter, but over time, I realized that my true passion lay in technology. So, I took the leap and started learning everything I could about coding and web design. It wasn't easy, and there were moments when I felt overwhelmed, but I persevered. Today, I am proud to say that I am a web developer and love every moment of my work.

In conclusion, I am an adventurous and curious individual who is passionate about technology, nature, and cooking. I am excited to continue growing and learning, both as a person and as a professional.
